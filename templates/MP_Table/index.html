If you are a victim, or are concerned about someone who might be, contact the National Human Trafficking Hotline at 888-373-7888 or text "BEFREE" to 233733.

How Can I Support The End Of Human Trafficking?

Pay close attention to the signs. If you know someone with untreated injuries, works excessive hours with little pay, does not possess their legal/travel documents, acts fearful, has an older boyfriend/girlfriend, allegedly owes money to employer, or has tattoos on their neck or lower back, this may be a sign of Human Trafficking. Pay close attention if someone you know has a combination of these signs, and act accordingly.
